Yorkshire

Yorkshire is arguably one of the most dynamic and vast counties in the country. Spanning nearly 3 million acres. She is both a culturally and geographically diverse region and is steeped in rich history and richer rides. The county is full of incredible and unforgiving climbs such as Lofthouse and Buttertubs Pass.

EYUP! Is a performance focussed premium cycle wear brand inspired by Yorkshire, we draw our inspiration from the unforgettable landscape a mix with good ol’, no messin’ Yorkshire simplicity.
